# Grais.ai Financial Projections

## 3-Year Financial Outlook (2025-2027)

### Revenue Model & Assumptions

**Pricing Tiers:**

- **Freemium:** $0 (30 messages/day) - Acquisition & viral growth
- **PRO:** $9/month (Unlimited + personal memory) - Target margin: 80%
- **Teams:** $16/seat/month (Shared memory, admin console) - Target margin: 78%
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing (SSO, analytics, support SLA) - Target margin: 75%
- **Data-Insights API (2027):** Usage-based pricing - Target margin: 85%

### Key Assumptions

**Conversion Rates:**

- Year 1 (2025): 0.5% freemium to paid conversion
- Year 2 (2026): 1.0% freemium to paid conversion
- Year 3 (2027): 1.2% freemium to paid conversion

**Customer Retention:**

- Net revenue retention: 120% annually
- Logo churn target: <3% by Year 3
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): <$25 for PRO users

**Cost Structure:**

- Token cost decline: -20% year-over-year
- Gross margin trajectory: >75% at scale
- Cloud-native infrastructure with horizontal scaling
